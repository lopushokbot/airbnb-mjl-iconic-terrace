The edge — space & terrace, not a view

This unit has no view (ground floor, courtyard-facing). Don't compete on view with Marina / JBR / Port de la Mer listings — you'll lose. Compete on what's genuinely scarce in MJL: a 91.5 m² floor plan with a large private hedged terrace and direct pool access, in Dubai's best family-tourism location.

MJL puts guests within a 5-minute walk of Burj Al Arab, Souk Madinat Jumeirah, Wild Wadi and the Madinat waterways — it is the family-and-couples base in Dubai. The terrace is this listing's "view equivalent": photograph it at golden hour, set for outdoor dining, and lead every listing photo with it.

Address the ground-floor objection head-on in the copy: "private hedged terrace, no through-traffic, steps from the pool." Turn the perceived negative into the headline feature.

Actions to take this week

  1. Get the first reviews — top priority. The listing is stuck in the discounted "Now" column until it has reviews. Price to fill, message guests day-of-checkout for a review, and aim for the first 5 reviews fast. Each one is worth ~AED 40–60/night going forward.
  2. Hero the terrace in photos. Lead with the private terrace shot at golden hour, set for outdoor dining. Add a wide shot showing the 91.5 m² space. The terrace is your "view" — show it first.
  3. Title built on space + location. Something like "MJL · Huge Private Terrace · Walk to Burj Al Arab" — bookers search by landmark and outdoor space, neither of which most MJL comps offer.
  4. Hold the summer floor at AED 390. Live July rate is ~AED 419–503/night — slightly above floor. Through Jul–Aug, drop toward 390 and lean on the −22% monthly discount to capture long stays.
  5. Block Dec 30 – Jan 1 now at AED 2,200 with a 4-night minimum. Never discount NYE — it's the single highest-value window of the year.
  6. Turn off Smart Pricing. Use this seasonal table. Smart Pricing under-prices peaks and over-discounts a new listing's low season.
  7. Set length-of-stay discounts: 7+ nights −10%, 14+ −15%, 28+ −22% — essential for filling summer and Ramadan.
